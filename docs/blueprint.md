# **App Name**: Generals Plumbing

## Core Features:

- Staff Clock-in/out: Allow staff to easily record their start and end times for work shifts via a simple interface.
- Timesheet & Reporting: View and export comprehensive timesheets for individual staff or teams, with totals calculated for payroll.
- Inventory Item Catalog: Manage a master list of all plumbing parts, tools, and supplies, including adding new items and editing existing details.
- Inventory Stock Levels: Track current quantities of all inventory items in real-time and set thresholds for low stock alerts.
- Dispatch Log Creation: Record items dispatched from inventory for specific jobs or assigned to individual technicians.
- Usage History & Analytics: Review historical data on item usage, linking dispensed items to jobs or technicians for accountability and planning.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A stable, professional deep blue (#297CB0), representing reliability and trust. Hue: 200, Saturation: 60%, Lightness: 40%.
- Background color: A very light, almost white blue-grey (#EBF3F5), promoting clarity and focus. Hue: 200, Saturation: 20%, Lightness: 95%.
- Accent color: A subtle, calm green-blue (#3C9F8A), providing a harmonious contrast for calls to action or highlights. Hue: 170, Saturation: 50%, Lightness: 45%.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a clean and modern sans-serif font, chosen for its excellent readability and neutral, professional aesthetic, suitable for detailed data display and general text.
- Utilize crisp, outlined icons that clearly represent their function (e.g., clock for check-in/out, wrench for tools, stacked boxes for inventory) to ensure quick recognition.
- Implement a clear, modular layout with distinct sections for different functionalities, ensuring efficient data input and review, especially on smaller mobile devices.
- Incorporate subtle and functional animations, such as gentle fades on screen transitions and subtle hovers on interactive elements, to enhance user experience without distraction.
